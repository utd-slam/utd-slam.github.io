Alzheimer's and Music Study

We are enrolling patients with Alzheimer’s Disease for a study investigating the long-term clinical efficacy of a music-based intervention called SoRhythm on slowing the progression of Alzheimer’s Disease symptoms including memory and language impairment. Those who participate will be asked to come in person to the Callier Center Richardson for two sessions which take 2 hours each. In these sessions, those who participate will receive cognitive assessments, a hearing test, and will listen to SoRhythm while receiving a brain recording. After all of the in person sessions are complete, those who participate will be compensated for their time.
